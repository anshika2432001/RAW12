Fever

Symptoms:

  • Chills and shivering
  • Headache
  • Muscle aches
  • Loss of appetite
  • Irritability
  • Dehydration
  • General weakness

Treatment:

  • Paracetamol

    • paracetamol helps to reduce fever and to relieve a headache with mild to moderate pain.

  • Aspirin

    • Aspirin is an analgesic and antipyretic, prescribed for pain, heart attack and fever.

  • Docosanol

    • Docosanol is a saturated fatty alcohol, prescribed for cold sores or fever blisters
